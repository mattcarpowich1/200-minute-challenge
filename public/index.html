My name is Matt Carpowich. If you are reading this, you may be in need of a ReactJS Developer. In my early web development days I learned of the pseudo-rivalry between Angular and React. Eventually I decided that I would try and learn React. Why? It turns out that I also produce and compose music - I have a fascination with sound. The word "React" flows off the tongue in an effortless manner, while "Angular" sounds like the mumbling of someone with a mouthful of undercooked Filet Mignon.

I prefer macOS.
